                     12, 2022. <a href="/classes/register/index.php">Register for classes</a> by using <a href="https://www.sjsu.edu/sfs/MyScheduler/index.html">MyScheduler</a>, which will generate every possible schedule for you so you can maximize your course
                     selections and remain on track for a timely graduation.&nbsp;</p>
